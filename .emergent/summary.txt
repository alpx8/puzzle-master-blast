**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
-   **Core Gameplay:** A block-placing puzzle game on a 10x10 grid. Clearing full rows or columns earns points. The game ends when a player can no longer place any of the three offered blocks.
-   **Visuals & Effects:**
    -   Blocks should be bright, colorful, and have a 3D effect.

    -   A New High Score celebration with unique visuals and sound.
-   **Audio & Haptics:**
    -   Sound effects for block placement, clearing lines, game over, and new high score.
    -   A mute button to disable all sounds.
    -   Vibration on combo clears.
-   **Multiplayer Mode (as specified by user in chat 398):**
    -   A lobby with separate lists for public and password-protected rooms.
    -   Users can create new rooms, either public or private (with a password).
    -   Gameplay is real-time and simultaneous for both players in a room.
    -   When one player runs out of moves, their game ends, and they are returned to the menu. The other player continues until they also run out of moves.
    -   A Game History or Pending Games section where players can see the final results (Win/Loss) of their matches.
-   **Features:**
    -   A leaderboard for highest scores.
    -   A system for random daily quests (UI is present, full implementation is pending).
